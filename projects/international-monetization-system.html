International Monetization System

GRAPHIC 2025

International creator monetization platform, similar to Google AdSense, enabling cross-border payments and revenue distribution

Overview

The International Monetization System powers GRAPHIC's core business: a digital asset-based donation platform for creators. Fans can purchase virtual currency to support their favorite creators through donations. Creators can then convert these digital assets into real money, creating a sustainable revenue stream.

The system manages the entire monetization flow: tracking how digital assets are transferred from supporters to creators, applying real-time exchange rates for accurate cross-border settlements, and ensuring transparent financial auditing throughout the process. This enables creators worldwide to receive fair compensation regardless of geographic or currency boundaries.

Key Features

  • Cross-border donation support with country-specific currency units
  • Virtual currency system with recharge mechanism
  • Real-time exchange rate integration for settlement
  • Asset management audit system for financial transparency
  • Automated revenue distribution with Wise integration
  • Multi-currency payment processing and tracking

Accounting System

The system implements a double-entry bookkeeping system to ensure financial accuracy and transparency. Every transaction follows a voucher-based recording process.

Voucher System

  • Deposit vouchers: Record incoming funds
  • Withdrawal vouchers: Record outgoing funds

Double-Entry Bookkeeping

Each voucher is recorded in the ledger using double-entry bookkeeping method, with corresponding debit and credit entries to maintain financial balance and integrity.

This double-entry system enables comprehensive auditing capabilities:

  • Balance verification: Sum of debits and credits must always equal zero
  • Transaction traceability: Complete audit trail for every financial movement
  • Data integrity: Hash-based validation prevents tampering with historical records
  • Error detection: Automatic identification of accounting discrepancies

When a user initiates a purchase, the system creates and tracks a payment transaction by method, processes it through the external provider, generates a deposit voucher upon success, and fulfills the product delivery via double-entry bookkeeping.

Exchange Rate System

When creators settle earnings in different currencies, the system applies the market exchange rate from the time of the original purchase. Exchange rates are collected and stored every 15 minutes, and each credit transaction in the ledger includes an exchange rate snapshot to ensure accurate settlement calculations regardless of when the withdrawal occurs.

How It Works

  • Exchange rates are automatically fetched and stored every 15 minutes
  • Each credit entry in the ledger records the exchange rate at purchase time
  • During settlement, the system retrieves the original exchange rate snapshot
  • Credits are converted to the destination currency using the recorded rate

International Payout System

For cross-border payments from Korea to countries like Japan and the United States, the system integrates with Wise to facilitate international transfers. The payout process automatically calculates country-specific taxes and transaction fees, deducting them from the settlement amount before transfer. Upon successful completion, the system generates and sends a receipt documenting the transaction details.

Audit System

The audit system continuously monitors the monetization platform to ensure integrity, accuracy, and prevent fraud. It performs automated verification across all financial transactions and generates regular reports for compliance and anomaly detection.

Ledger Integrity Verification

  • Hash chain validation to detect voucher tampering
  • Debit/credit balance verification (sum must equal zero)
  • Sequential voucher order consistency checks

Balance Reconciliation

  • Total user and creator credit balance verification
  • Comparison between actual deposits and system-issued credits
  • Comparison between withdrawals and credit redemptions

Exchange Rate Validation

  • Verification that recorded rates fall within actual market rate ranges
  • Detection of missing exchange rate data

Transaction Traceability

  • End-to-end tracking from payment to settlement
  • Identification of unsettled items
  • Monitoring of delayed settlement cases

Tax & Fee Calculation Verification

  • Accuracy verification of country-specific tax rates
  • Detection of fee calculation errors

Automated Audit Reports

  • Daily and monthly financial status summaries
  • Anomalous transaction detection and alerts
  • Automatic reporting of discrepancies
